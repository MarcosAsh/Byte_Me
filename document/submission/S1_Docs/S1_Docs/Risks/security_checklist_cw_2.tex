\section*{Overview}

This document lists the security measures in place for the final Byte Me system, along with outstanding items and the reasoning for why they fall outside the current scope. 22 items are implemented. 6 are not implemented, all with documented justification.

\section*{Authentication}

\begin{longtable}{|p{4.5cm}|p{2.5cm}|p{7cm}|}
\hline
\textbf{Item} & \textbf{Status} & \textbf{Notes} \\
\hline
Password hashing & \done & BCrypt via Spring Security \texttt{BCryptPasswordEncoder} \\
\hline
JWT token authentication & \done & HMAC-SHA signed. Claims include \texttt{userId}, \texttt{email}, \texttt{role} \\
\hline
Token expiration & \done & 24 hours, configurable via \texttt{JWT\_EXPIRATION} env var \\
\hline
Token refresh mechanism & \notimpl & Users must re-login after expiry. Acceptable for coursework scope \\
\hline
Rate limiting on login & \notimpl & Brute force possible in theory. Would need Spring Rate Limiter or an API gateway for production \\
\hline
Account lockout after failed attempts & \notimpl & Not required for current scope \\
\hline
\end{longtable}

\section*{Authorisation}

\begin{longtable}{|p{4.5cm}|p{2.5cm}|p{7cm}|}
\hline
\textbf{Item} & \textbf{Status} & \textbf{Notes} \\
\hline
Role-based access control & \done & Two roles: \texttt{SELLER}, \texttt{ORG\_ADMIN}. Enforced at controller level \\
\hline
Ownership validation & \done & Every protected endpoint verifies the requesting user owns the resource (e.g.\ a seller can only modify their own bundles) \\
\hline
Database-level role enforcement & \done & Triggers \texttt{seller\_role\_check} and \texttt{organisation\_role\_check} prevent role mismatches at insert \\
\hline
Public endpoint whitelist & \done & \texttt{SecurityConfig} permits \texttt{/api/auth/**}, \texttt{GET /api/bundles}, \texttt{GET /api/categories}, \texttt{GET /api/gamification/badges} \\
\hline
\end{longtable}

\section*{Data Protection}

\begin{longtable}{|p{4.5cm}|p{2.5cm}|p{7cm}|}
\hline
\textbf{Item} & \textbf{Status} & \textbf{Notes} \\
\hline
Secrets in environment variables & \done & \texttt{DB\_URL}, \texttt{DB\_USERNAME}, \texttt{DB\_PASSWORD}, \texttt{JWT\_SECRET} all via env vars, not in code \\
\hline
\texttt{.env.example} provided (no real secrets) & \done & Template file in repo root and \texttt{backend/} \\
\hline
\texttt{.gitignore} covers \texttt{.env} files & \done & \texttt{.env} excluded from version control \\
\hline
Encrypted database connections & \done & Railway PostgreSQL uses TLS by default \\
\hline
Claim codes hashed & \done & BCrypt hashed; only the last 4 digits shown to the user \\
\hline
CORS configuration & \done & Allowed origins configurable via \texttt{CORS\_ORIGINS} env var \\
\hline
CSRF protection & \disabled & Appropriate for a stateless JWT API with no cookie-based auth \\
\hline
\end{longtable}

\section*{Input Validation}

\begin{longtable}{|p{4.5cm}|p{2.5cm}|p{7cm}|}
\hline
\textbf{Item} & \textbf{Status} & \textbf{Notes} \\
\hline
Frontend email validation & \done & Format check on \texttt{@} and domain \\
\hline
Frontend password minimum length & \done & 6 characters minimum \\
\hline
Frontend required field checks & \done & All forms validate before submission \\
\hline
Backend availability check before reservation & \done & Trigger verifies bundle is \texttt{ACTIVE}, pickup window has not ended, and quantity is available \\

Database constraints on business rules & \done & 7+ \texttt{CHECK} constraints (price non-negative, discount 0--100, quantity reserved $\leq$ total, pickup end $>$ start, weight non-negative) \\

XSS prevention & \done & React auto-escapes rendered content by default \\

\section*{Summary}

22 items are implemented. 6 are not implemented. All 6 fall outside the coursework scope for one of two reasons: they would only be necessary for a production deployment handling real user data (rate limiting, account lockout, audit logging, dependency scanning, staging environment), or the mitigation is contractual rather than technical and is covered by documentation (allergen verification). Each is noted in the risk register with a justification.
